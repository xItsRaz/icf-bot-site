- **Community participation:** Engaging in server discussions, events, and activities related to the ICF clan and Arma 3 simulation.

## 5. Moderation and Enforcement

The ICF Bot provides staff with moderation tools. By being a server member, you acknowledge and accept that:

### 5.1 Moderation Actions

Staff may take the following actions against your account at their discretion:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **`/warn`** | A warning is issued and permanently added to your record |
| **`/timeout`** | You are temporarily muted (communication disabled) for a set duration |
| **`/kick`** | You are removed from the server (you may re-join unless banned) |
| **`/ban`** | You are permanently removed and blocked from the server |
| **`/bulkdel`** | Staff may bulk-delete messages, including yours, from any channel |

### 5.2 Moderation Logs

All moderation actions are logged in private staff channels. Warning counts, message counts, invite counts, and timeout status are accessible to moderators via the `/lookup` command.

### 5.3 Appeal Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken unfairly, you may appeal by:
1. Contacting a senior staff member via Discord DM.
2. Opening an appeal ticket if you still have access to the server.

Appeals are reviewed at the discretion of ICF leadership. Decisions made by the server owner are final.

## 6. Ticket System

By opening a support ticket you acknowledge:

- **A private channel is created** visible only to you and relevant staff roles.
- **Staff may add additional users** to your ticket at their discretion.
- **When the ticket is closed**, a full transcript of all messages in the ticket channel (including your messages, usernames, and timestamps) is automatically saved in a private staff log channel.
- **Transcripts are retained** indefinitely unless manually deleted by an administrator.
- **Do not share sensitive personal information** (real name, home address, phone number, government ID, financial data) inside tickets. We are not responsible for any personal data you voluntarily share.

Ticket transcripts may be reviewed by ICF leadership for quality assurance, dispute resolution, or moderation purposes.

## 7. Birthday Feature

The `/birthday` command is entirely **voluntary**. By using `/birthday set` you:

- Consent to the storage of your Discord User ID and the birthday date (day, month, year) you provide in the ICF Bot's database.
- Consent to the bot posting a public birthday announcement in the designated birthdays channel on your birthday.
- Consent to the bot temporarily updating your server nickname to include your age on your birthday (e.g., `Username 25`). This requires the bot to have the **Manage Nicknames** permission over your account.

You may withdraw this consent at any time by using `/birthday remove`, which immediately deletes your birthday record from the database.
