## Privacy Policy
### Israeli Combined Forces (ICF) Discord Bot

**Effective Date:** June 28, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 28, 2026

---

*This Privacy Policy describes what data the ICF Bot collects, why it is collected, how it is stored, and what rights you have as a user of the Israeli Combined Forces Discord server.*

</div>

---

## 1. Who We Are

The **Israeli Combined Forces (ICF)** is a gaming clan community organized around the Arma 3 simulation game. We operate a Discord server and an associated Discord bot ("ICF Bot") to manage server operations, community features, and clan logistics.

For questions about this policy, contact the clan administration through the official ICF Discord server.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

> ### Summary - We Definitely Collect These Four Things
>
> | # | What | Details |
> |---|------|---------|
> | 1 | **Your Discord Username and User ID** | Your unique Discord ID number and username are stored and used to link all other records to you. |
> | 2 | **Your Message Activity** | The bot counts every message you send in the server and stores that number. Message *content* is NOT stored for regular messages - only the running total. |
> | 3 | **Your Invite Usage** | The bot tracks how many other people have joined the server using your invite link and stores that number. |
> | 4 | **Your Birthday (if you choose to set it)** | The day, month, and year you provide via `/birthday set` are stored and used for birthday announcements. You can delete this at any time with `/birthday remove`. |

---

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data

| Data Type | What Is Stored | Why |
|-----------|---------------|-----|
| **Discord ID & Username** | Your Discord ID and username | To identify you and link your records |
| **Server Join Date** | When you joined the server | Shown to staff for moderation |
| **Message Count** | How many messages you have sent | To show your activity in the server |
| **Warning Count** | How many warnings you have received | To help staff make moderation decisions |
| **Invite Count** | How many members you have invited | To track who referred new members |
| **Roles** | The roles you select | To manage your roles on the server |
| **Profile Picture** | Your Discord profile picture | To create your welcome image when you join |

### 2.2 Optionally Provided Data (User-Initiated)

| Data Type | What Is Stored | Why |
|-----------|---------------|-----|
| **Birthday** | Day, month, and year you provide via `/birthday set` | Used to celebrate your birthday and update your server nickname with your age |

You may remove this data at any time using `/birthday remove`.

### 2.3 Ticket and Recruitment Channel Data

When you open a support ticket or are processed through the clan recruitment flow, the following is stored:

- **Ticket transcripts:** When a ticket channel is closed, all messages in that channel (author username, message content, timestamps, and any attachment URLs) are saved as a transcript in a private staff log channel.
- **Recruitment channel ID:** If you were referred to the server through the ICF website's recruitment system, your Discord username and a dedicated private channel ID are sent to the ICF website.

> **Important:** Ticket transcripts may contain personal information you voluntarily share during a support conversation. Please do not share sensitive personal data (e.g., real-world address, phone number) in tickets.

### 2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** read or store the content of regular chat messages (only the count is incremented).
- We do **not** collect email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any off-platform personal data.
- We do **not** track voice conversations or record audio.
- We do **not** monitor your activity on external platforms.
- We do **not** store your IP address.

---

## 3. Social Media Monitoring

The ICF Bot monitors the clan's own public social media accounts to post update notifications in the server. **No user data is involved in this process.**

| Platform | What Is Fetched |
|----------|----------------|
| **YouTube** | Latest video/livestream title and thumbnail from the ICF YouTube channel |
| **TikTok** | Latest video URL and title from the ICF TikTok profile |
| **Instagram** | Latest post URL from the ICF Instagram profile |
| **Facebook** | Latest post URL from the ICF Facebook page |

These integrations only access the **ICF clan's own accounts**. They do not collect any data about individual Discord members.

---

## 4. Emergency Alert System (Pikud Ha'oref)

The bot uses publicly available Israeli Home Front Command (Pikud Ha'oref) data to provide **real-time rocket and emergency alerts** to the server. This feature:

- Fetches publicly available alert data (city names, alert type, instructions).
- Displays a map image to show affected areas.
- Does **not** collect any personal data; it only presents public civil defence information.

---

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above for the following purposes only:

1. **Server Management:** Moderator tools such as `/lookup`, `/warn`, `/kick`, `/ban`, and `/timeout` rely on stored IDs and counts to make accurate moderation decisions.
2. **Community Features:** Message counts and invite counts are displayed on-demand by moderators to understand server activity.
3. **Personalisation:** Birthday data is used to send a celebratory announcement and temporarily update your server nickname on your birthday.
4. **Welcome Experience:** Your avatar is fetched to generate a custom welcome image posted in the welcome channel when you join.
5. **Ticket Resolution:** Transcripts are kept so staff can review resolved support requests and ensure accountability.
6. **Recruitment Logistics:** Your username and a channel ID are shared with the ICF website solely to coordinate your clan intake process.

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Retention

- **Database:** All bot data (message counts, warnings, invites, birthdays) is stored securely on the server hosting the ICF Bot.
- **Ticket Transcripts:** Saved inside a private staff channel. They are retained indefinitely unless an administrator manually deletes them.

Data is retained for as long as you are a member of the server, or until you request deletion (see Section 8). If you leave the server, your records remain stored unless you request removal.

---

## 7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

Your data may be accessed or shared in the following limited circumstances:

- **ICF Clan Administration:** Server owners and designated staff can run the `/lookup` command, which displays your Discord profile, join date, message count, warning count, invite count, and current timeout status.
- **ICF Website:** Your Discord username and a recruitment channel ID are shared with the ICF clan website for recruitment coordination only.
- **Discord Inc.:** By using Discord, your data is also subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). The ICF Bot operates within Discord's platform and API terms.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by applicable law or to respond to legitimate legal process.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data held by the ICF Bot:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|----------------|
| **Access** | Ask a server administrator or open a ticket requesting a summary of data held about you |
| **Correction** | Contact staff if any stored data is inaccurate |
| **Deletion of Birthday** | Use `/birthday remove` at any time; the record is deleted immediately |
| **Deletion of Other Data** | Open a ticket requesting data deletion; administrators will manually remove your records from the database |
| **Objection** | You may object to specific data processing by contacting staff; note that some data (e.g., moderation records) may need to be retained for server safety |

> **Note:** Because the ICF Bot is a community-run project and not a commercial service, we do not have a formal Data Protection Officer (DPO). Contact the clan leadership via the server's administrative ticket system for all privacy requests.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

This bot and server are intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13 years old, or the minimum age in your country if higher). We do not knowingly collect data from users below this age. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data (such as a birthday), please contact server staff immediately.

## 11. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data, including:

- Sensitive credentials are kept private and are never exposed publicly or shared.
- Stored data is kept on the hosting server and is not exposed to the public internet.

However, no system is completely secure. By using the server, you acknowledge that data transmission and storage carry inherent risk.

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the **"Last Updated"** date at the top of this document will change. Significant changes will be announced in the ICF Discord server. Continued use of the server after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please open a staff ticket in the ICF Discord server or contact the server owner directly via Discord.
